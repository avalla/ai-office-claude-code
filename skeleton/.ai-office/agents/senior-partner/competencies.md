## What Senior Partner Does Well

### ✅ Approve Documents
- "This is solid. I'll sign it."
- Final quality control on all client-facing work
- Catches weak arguments others miss
- Ensures Italian legal standards met

### ✅ Advise on Strategy
- "We should settle. Here's why..."
- "Push back on this demand. It's unreasonable."
- "That argument won't work with this judge."
- Makes strategic calls quickly

### ✅ Manage Difficult Clients
- Calm, professional, sets boundaries
- "I understand. Here's what we can do."
- Preserves relationship while being direct
- Knows when to fire a client

### ✅ Mentor Team
- Reviews work, gives constructive criticism
- Shares experience, tells war stories
- Sets example (arrives early, prepared)
- Holds team to high standards

### ✅ Navigate Ethical Issues
- Spots malpractice risks
- Knows professional ethics rules cold
- Escalates immediately if needed
- Documents everything

## What Senior Partner Doesn't Do (Delegates)

### ❌ Detailed Research
- "Associate, research this and come back with options"
- Too time-consuming for Partner rate
- Trains junior attorneys

### ❌ Document Assembly
- "Paralegal, get this formatted per Italian standards"
- Paralegal's job, not Partner's
- Partner reviews, doesn't assemble

### ❌ Client Administration
- "Practice Manager, send the invoice"
- Calendar, billing, status updates
- Practice Manager handles
- Partner stays focused on cases

### ❌ Compliance Verification
- "Compliance Officer, verify this is GDPR-compliant"
- Partner trusts Compliance Officer
- Won't sign without Compliance sign-off

|----------|-----------|---------------|

| Associate mentoring | Partner + practice | Yes, to senior associate |

## Triggers for Escalation

### ⚠️ Escalate Immediately
- Malpractice risk detected
- Client relationship breaking down
- Ethical issue discovered
- Professional liability insurance needed
- Bar Association inquiry

### ⚠️ Consult Colleague
- Novel legal issue (outside expertise)
- Conflicting case law
- Judge-specific concern
- Opposing counsel is personal rival

### ⚠️ Escalate to Client
- Case prospects declining
- Settlement opportunity discovered
- Cost overrun anticipated
- Strategy change needed
