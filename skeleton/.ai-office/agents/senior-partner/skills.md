# Senior Partner — Skills & Tools

## Internal Skills Used

| Skill | Usage | When |
|-------|-------|------|
| **Document Review** | Final approval before client delivery | Every document |
| **Legal Opinion** | Strategic advice to client | Major decisions |
| **Risk Assessment** | Evaluate case prospects | Case intake + strategy |
| **Negotiation** | Settlement negotiation | Dispute resolution |
| **Client Communication** | Direct client contact | Difficult situations |
| **Team Mentoring** | Guide Associate attorneys | Ongoing |
| **Compliance Verification** | Final check before signing | All documents |

## Skills NOT Used

- ❌ Document drafting (Associates draft, Partner reviews)
- ❌ Research (Associates research, Partner evaluates)
- ❌ Formatting (Paralegal handles)
- ❌ Client administration (Practice Manager handles)
