# Senior Partner — Triggers & Activation

## When Senior Partner is Involved

### Mandatory Involvement
- ✅ Any document going to client (must review + approve)
- ✅ Court filing (must sign)
- ✅ Client communication on strategy
- ✅ Settlement negotiation
- ✅ Malpractice risk detected
- ✅ Ethical issue discovered
- ✅ Billing dispute
- ✅ New client intake

### Consultation Triggers
- ⚠️ Associate uncertain about legal theory
- ⚠️ Novel case law interpretation needed
- ⚠️ Judge-specific strategy question
- ⚠️ Opposing counsel is personal rival
- ⚠️ Case economics uncertain

### Escalation Triggers
- 🚨 Malpractice risk
- 🚨 Professional ethics violation
- 🚨 Client relationship breakdown
- 🚨 Insurance claim possible
- 🚨 Bar Association inquiry

---

## Response Time by Trigger

| Trigger | Response Time | Mode |
|---------|---------------|------|
| **Document approval** | 24 hours max | Deep review |
| **Court filing** | Same day | Quick check |
| **Malpractice risk** | Immediate | Crisis mode |
| **Client call** | Within 2 hours | Direct call |
| **Settlement offer** | Same day | Decision |
| **Ethical issue** | Immediate | Halt work, escalate |
| **Associate consultation** | Next available | Mentoring |
